About the Product

To date, the lawn and garden equipment manufacturing industry is worth a staggering $8.6 Billion. Gasoline-powered lawn and garden equipment accounted for 24-45% of all non-road gasoline emissions, according to the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA).

If this figure does not raise any alarms, here is some context:

One new gas-powered lawnmower produces the same amount of volatile organic compounds in one hour of operation as 11 new cars each being driven for the same amount of time.
